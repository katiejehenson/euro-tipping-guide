🌍 Universal Guide: Private Transfers & Tour Guides

General benchmarks that apply broadly across Europe

✈️Airport / Port Transfer
€5–10 / £5–10
One-way, standard sedan or van
🚐Half-Day Private Transfer
€10–15 / £10–15
3–5 hour hire with dedicated driver
🚗Full-Day Private Driver
€15–25 / £15–25
8+ hours; includes waiting time and multiple stops
🗺️Multi-Day Driver
€15–20/day / £15–20/day
Country touring, road trips; tip at end of last day
🎙️Group Tour Guide
€3–8 per person
Shared walking or bus tour, half to full day
🎒Private Guide — Half Day
€15–25 per person
3–4 hours, bespoke tour
🏛️Private Guide — Full Day
€20–40 per person
6–8 hours; history, wine, food, nature, etc.
🍷Specialist / Expert Guide
€30–50+ per person
Sommeliers, archaeologists, former curators, etc.

💡 Universal Etiquette Tips

Always tip in cash
Card tips often don't reach staff due to pooling or processing fees.
Tip at the end of service
For multi-day guides or drivers, present the tip on the final day.
Carry small notes
€5, €10, and £5 notes are ideal for discreet, personal tipping.
Be discreet
A folded note in a handshake is more gracious than waving money publicly.
Check the bill first
Service charge may already be included — look for 'servizio', 'service compris', or 'service charge'.
It's always optional
No tip is ever legally required in Europe. It is a gesture of appreciation.
